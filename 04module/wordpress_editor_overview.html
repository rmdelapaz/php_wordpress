The Evolution of WordPress Content Editing

WordPress has transformed from a simple blogging platform to a powerful content management system, and its editors have evolved to meet changing needs. Understanding both editors helps you choose the right tool for each project.

Historical Context
The Classic Editor served WordPress for 15 years (2003-2018). Gutenberg, introduced in WordPress 5.0 (December 2018), represents the future of WordPress content creation with its block-based approach.
